YOU HAVE REACHED THE PRESENT

The pattern continues.

Writing made the tax ledger possible; the ledger made the state possible; the state made the census possible — and every age since has rediscovered the same truth: power rests on the regulation of labor and information. The telegraph begat the wiretap, the camera begat the privacy tort, the platform begat the data law, and the machine that writes begat the questions this museum cannot yet answer.
